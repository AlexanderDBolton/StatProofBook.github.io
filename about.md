This website is [generated from a public repository](https://github.com/StatProofBook/StatProofBook.github.io) and everyone is [welcome to contribute](https://statproofbook.github.io/contribute/). Please [follow us on Twitter](https://twitter.com/statproofbook) to stay up to date on new developments and new proofs being added to the archive.

**The Book of Statistical Proofs** is maintained by *Joram Soch* ([ORCID](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8879-5666), [GitHub](https://github.com/JoramSoch), [Twitter](https://twitter.com/JoramSoch)).
